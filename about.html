About Qatar Meat

Based in Qatar and Sri Lanka, Qatar Meat is an international trading and supplying company that prides itself in distributing premium cuts of live, fresh and frozen meat, and seafood products at the most competitive prices. Our company has been in the business since 2008 and we are committed to providing exceptional service. This has been the driving force behind the success of our company and we look forward to serving our clients in the future as well.

VISION AND MISSION

Our values are more than just words - they are incorporated in every aspect of what we do. It reflects what we stand for and they symbolize the work we do everyday to achieve and fulfill our goal of being the best in what we do - providing the Middle East with premium quality meat, seafood and specialty food products from sustainable sources from around the world.

Vision

We strive to provide exceptional customer service at every stage through quality products, efficient services and excellent communication to ensure that our customers are satisfied and delighted.

Mission

We talk with our actions and we strive for nothing but the best. We constantly challenge ourselves to improve and enhance the services we provide to our valuable customers.
